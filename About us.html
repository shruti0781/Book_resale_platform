About Us

"Founded with the mission to make reading affordable and sustainable, Resale Books is India’s trusted marketplace for buying and selling pre-loved books. From academic textbooks to timeless novels, we connect readers and students with quality books at a fraction of the original cost. We believe that every book deserves a second life. By re-homing books, we not only make knowledge accessible but also help reduce paper waste and protect the environment. Our platform is built to make the process simple—list your book in minutes, connect with genuine buyers, and enjoy hassle-free pickup and delivery services.A community marketplace where students,teachers and readers resell their old books-making education cheaper and greener."
